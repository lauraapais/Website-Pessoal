Crimes Exemplares

Graphic Design
Interaction Design

CONTEXT
Academic Project — MDM
Collaboration with Fábio Gouveia and João Neves

ADVISED BY
Ana Boavida, Luís Lucas Pereira

Crimes Exemplares (Exemplary Crimes) is a literary work, composed of small texts, that report various crimes, in the most varied forms and situations. Mostly surreal in nature. These criminal acts have impulses of extreme violence, without an ethical or moral nature, and, with its sort of comic speech, it enhances the absurd. Although it is impossible to calculate its veracity, these short stories challenge the reader to ask what is the limit of mental sanity and what can trigger the unreasonability of this type of acts.

Two transmedia objects of the stories were developed, a board game and an augmented reality poster. In both the objects developed, these contrasting dualities were explored: the sobriety and the madness, the animal and the innocent, the veracity and the unbelief, supported by elements that create a connection to the years of the crimes.

TOOLS/LANGUAGES
