Project Arq

Graphic Design
2022

CONTEXT
Apresentação do Projeto de Arquitetura, Darq, UC

Elaboration of Communication Materials for the event Apresentação do Projeto de Arquitetura, where the restoration project of the Department of Architecture (DArq) will be presented.

Inspired by the characteristic ceiling due to its triangular shape, a poster, stories and posts for Facebook and Instagram were made to publicize this event, where its main element is triangular.

TOOLS/LANGUAGES
Adobe Illustrator, InDesign, After Effects
