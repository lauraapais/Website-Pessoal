Tamara

Typography
Graphic Design
2022

CONTEXT
Academic Project — MDM
Collaboration with Francisca Calisto

ADVISED BY
Artur Rebelo, João Bicker

Tamara is a modular typography made of modules contained in signs and inscriptions from the streets of Coimbra.

The modules together, with large contrasts on their stems, remindes the Art Deco style. Therefore, designating the typography as Tamara honors Tamara de Lempicka, a Polish Art Deco painter.

TOOLS/LANGUAGES
Adobe Illustrator, InDesign, After Effects
