I’m Laura Pais, a designer currently based in Porto, Portugal. I have a Master’s and a Bachelor’s degree in Design and Multimedia from the University of Coimbra.


My main areas of interest are graphic design, web design, web development and typography. My working method focuses on the digital domain, taking advantage of my technological proficiency. I also have strong capabilities in producing editorial materials, combining digital and print design. I’m passionate about music, cinema, traveling, and designing cool stuff.
